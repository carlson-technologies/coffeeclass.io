[Supabase](https://supabase.io) (not *Super*base) is an open source alternative to [Firebase](https://firebase.google.com). As a long time user of Firebase, I was curious to see the differences between the two. In this article, I'll go over the pros and cons of Supabase and Firebase.

## No Charge for Reads and Writes

Right away, my first question when I heard about Supabase was, "What is the free tier for reads and writes?". Coming from Firebase where anything over 20k writes, 20k deletes, and 50k reads costs money, I assumed Supabase followed the same model. Hovewer, this is not the case! Reads, writes, and deletes are 100% free on their free tier.
